Ben and I became aquainted because we were both newcomers to the Michigan Marching Band tuba section in the 2024 season. We became closer friends later that year when we both joined the Michigan Hockey Band. Ben is incredibly caring, funny, and clever. He is very well known for his fantastic stories, wide interests, and his tendancy to dress a bit like a grandpa. In his free time, Ben arranges tunes for the hockey band, plays trombone in a jazz ensemble, and he is also the proud owner of a retired Blue Bus.
